Color Critters

Welcome to the Color Critters beta! Please read the brief instructions, and then take a look at the interactive below.

The world below is full of simple critters. When critters mate with one another, the child's color is a blend of its parents' colors. The world also contains a predator that eats critters.

The button at the top left starts and stops the world. Use the button next to it to switch between modes. There are two modes besides Observe mode. In Predator mode, try to max out your health by hunting critters with your mouse. But watch out: if you hunt unsuccessfully, you'll waste energy. In AUTO Predator mode, the computer hunts for you.

If you want to start over again, you'll have to refresh the page. You can also change the size of the world by resizing your window before you refresh.
